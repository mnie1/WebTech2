MUN in Central China

Central China conferences, because of the region's high educational standards as well as its socioeconomic advantages, is arguably the region with the most advanced MUN conferences. Provinces and municipalities of central China include but are not limited to Shanghai, Henan, Hebei, Jiangxi, Zhejiang, and Jiangsu.

The following are MUN conferences that are in Central China

  • Shanghai American School Model United Nations
  • Concordia International School Shanghai Model United Nations
  • PUXI Model United Nations
  • Ivy League Model United Nations China
  • Fudan University International Model United Nations
  • Nanjing International School Model United Nations
  • Suzhou Model United Nations
