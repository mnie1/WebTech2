MUN in Southern China

Southern China conferences have been very prevelant over the past two decades, especially with the rise of the Pearl River Delta region as well as Hong Kong's relevancy at the world stage. Provinces, municipalities, and territories of southern China include but are not limited to Guangdong, Hong Kong, Macau, Fujian, Guangxi, and Hainan.

The following are MUN conferences that are in Southern China

  • Pan-Pearl River Delta Model United Nations>
  • Hong Kong Secondary School Model United Nations Conference
  • Utahloy United Nations Education, Scientific, and Cultural Organization
  • Shekou International School Model United Nations
  • Xiamen Model United Nations
  • Hong Kong International School Model United Nations
  • City University of Hong Kong Model United Nations
