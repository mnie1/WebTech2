The following are my main interests, which are:

  1. City Pop
  2. Riding my Bike
  3. Watching and Analyzing Movies

Listening to City Pop

City pop is a sub-genre of pop music and it originated during the economic and technological boom in Japan during the 1980s-1990s.
I like listening to city pop because it captures a very complicated emotion of reminiscence and a longing for a past that never existed. City pop is also about a past that is also simultaneously "the future".
The duality of this is what interests me the most.

Riding the Bicycle

I always biked to school when I was in Shenzhen. The moments on a bicycle are moments of serenity.
Whenever I ride a bike, whether it'd be a long journey or a short one, I have to listen to music.
And during that time, when everyday scenes are immersed with music, the cycling doesn't seem like a transportation machine anymore, but more like a tool to make what you see a video. The constant change in scenes combined with the music is such an ecstatic experience.

Watching Movies and Film Analysis Videos

I really like watching movies, so much so that I find visual representation much more appealing than reading (although that may be an excuse for not wanting to read).
Films encapsulate the combination of video footage and sound, so whenever I found a particular movie scene that fit perfectly to the background soundtrack, I feel absolutely amazed and, at times, am inspired with awe.
