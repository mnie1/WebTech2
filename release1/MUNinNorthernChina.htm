MUN in Northern China

Northern China conferences are situated mostly in Beijing, but in other regions, they are fairly undeveloped. Provinces and municipalities of northern China include but are not limited to Beijing, Tianjin, Hebei, Jilin, HeiLongJiang, and Liaoning.

The following are MUN conferences that are in Northern China

  • Dulwich International Model United Nations Beijing
  • Beijing Model United Nations
  • Peking University National Model United Nations
  • Tianjin Model United Nations
  • Beijing International Model United Nations
  • Harvard Model United Nations China
  • Jilin University of Finance and Economics Model United Nations
